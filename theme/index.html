ABOUT ME

As a Robotics engineer I am specialized in Machine Learning with interests in mechanical engineering. After finishing my master at the University of Delft I was searching for the right path to take. After working some months, I came to realize that leveraging Artificial Intelligence to solve real-world problems at different companies was where my heart truly lies. I thrive in a dynamic and fast-paced flat organization that fosters a culture of innovation and encourages collaboration and helping each other. My goal is to continually expand my knowledge and expertise in MLOps, having complete knowledge of an efficient full machine learning pipeline.

I am 25 years old and currently living in Purmerend with my girlfriend. Apart from my professional pursuits, I have a passion for team sports. Where I actively participate in korfbal at www.kvbep.nl and enjoy spectating basketball and football matches in my free time.
